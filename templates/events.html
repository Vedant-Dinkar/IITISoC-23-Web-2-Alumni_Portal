Ceremonial Convocation of batch 2020 IIT Indore

IIT Indore hosted the Ceremonial Convocation of 2020 batch on December 28, 2022. Out of the total of 412 degree recipients, 170 were present at the ceremony. Jan 03, 2023

Proudly Made in India, Dozee's Contactless VS Measurement Technology receives USFDA Clearance

Dozee Contactless Vital Signs (VS) Measurement System has received US-FDA 510K clearance. FDA clearance for any technology is a symbol of the best global standards. Established in 2015, Bengaluru-based AI healthcare startup Dozee was set up by our alumnus, Mr Gaurav Parchani (BTech ME 2013). Mr Gaurav has also been featured in Forbes India 30 under 30. Dozee provides contactless health monitors that silently track heart, respiration, sleep patterns, stress levels, cardiac contractions, apnea, and more while sleeping. Its artificial intelligence algorithms enable early detection of any health deterioration. IIT Indore is exceptionally proud of its co-founder. We are confident that his accomplishment will inspire all of our students and propel us to greater levels.

Gaurav Parchani's Dozee Healthtech Goes Global!

Our alumnus, Mr Gaurav Parchani (BTech ME 2013) had come up with a startup, Dozee which is now India's first contactless remote patient monitoring device, that tracks chronic cardiac conditions, hypertension and respiratory diseases. Recently, Dozee was seen in the limelight when it had received an additional fundraise of Rs. 71 crore led by DoorDash's product and business helper Gokul Rajaram. The company added that the current funding round also saw participation from existing investors, including Prime Venture Partners, 3one4 Capital, YourNest, among other angel investors Mr Gaurav has also been featured in Forbes India 30 under 30. His remarkable contribution in revolutionizing contactless healthcare amidst the Coronavirus pandemic as the co-founder of Dozee has made IIT Indore very proud indeed. We believe his achievement will act as a stimulus to all of the students and help us soar to new heights.
