About Us

IIT Indore alumni form a vast and accomplished network spread across the globe. With a strong presence worldwide, our alumni are making significant contributions in academia, research, industry, entrepreneurship, and public service. Through events and programs, they actively engage with the institute, sharing their knowledge and experiences. The alumni directory serves as a valuable resource, connecting current students and fellow graduates. Inspiring stories of their achievements and success motivate the IIT Indore community. Regular news updates keep everyone informed about the latest accomplishments and initiatives of our esteemed alumni, further strengthening the bond between the institute and its graduates.
